To stay healthy or to improve health, older adults need to do two types of physical activity each week: aerobic and muscle-strengthening activity.
Examples of activities that require moderate effort for most people include:

1.walking fast.
2.doing water aerobics.
3.ballroom and line dancing.
4.riding a bike on level ground or with a few hills.
5.playing doubles tennis.
6.pushing a lawn mower.
7.canoeing.
8.volleyball.
9.carrying or moving heavy loads such as groceries.
10.activities that involve stepping and jumping such as dancing.
11.heavy gardening, such as digging or shovelling.
12.exercises that use your body weight for resistance, such as push-ups or sit-ups.
13.yoga.
14.lifting weights.
